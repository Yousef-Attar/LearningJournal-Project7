Hi there, my name is Yousef Attar and welcome to my learning journal

I'm a Computer Science student who started this journal to share my journey of learning, growth, and passion for technology. My goal is to become a skilled full stack web developer and build real-world applications that make a difference.

How it all started

In 2022, I began studying Computer Science at university. After completing my first year, I took a robotics course and became fascinated with automation, sensors, and physical computing.

But at the same time, I discovered a strong passion for web development and decided to dive deeper into that world. I joined the Frontend Developer Career Path on Scrimba, where I started building real projects and learning modern web tools.

Where I am now

After finishing the frontend path, I continued into the Full Stack Developer Career Path to learn backend technologies like Node.js, SQL, TypeScript, Express, and even AI integration with advanced React and Next.js.

I'm currently also working as a Coding and Robotics Teacher at BrightChamps, teaching students from different countries and helping them fall in love with programming the way I did.
